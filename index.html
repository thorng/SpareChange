What is Pocket Change?



  1. Pocket Change is a iOS Game philanthropic twist
  2. With Pocket Change , users can compete with friends from around the world to donate the most
  3. How does Pocket Change work?
    • Select the amount you want to donate
    • Press to donate however much you would like, while playing the mini game
    • At the end of the day, the charity organizations will face off
  4. The final outcome of playing?
    • The organization with the most supporters earns 75% of the total donation
    • The other charity will earn 25% of all of the donations.
    • Therefore every one will win at the end and enjoy a fun and competitive way to give back
